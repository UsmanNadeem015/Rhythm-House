Our Story

Rhythm House began as a small corner shop in 1995, born from a love of music and film. Over the years, we’ve grown into a beloved hub for collectors and casual fans alike, offering rare vinyl records, classic DVDs, and cinematic gems from every era.

Whether you’re hunting for a forgotten jazz masterpiece or the perfect movie night pick, our shelves are filled with nostalgia, passion, and history — all waiting for you.
